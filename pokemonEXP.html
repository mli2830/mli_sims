Exp Curve for Pokemon Go

Pokemon Go raised the Level cap to level 40. It just seems so hard to level up after level 20. I am just curious what the exp curve looks like. There is a exp table at serbebii.net, but tables are not good. This is a good example illustrating why pictures are better than tables when interpreting data. I should of scrape the table but too lazy.

lvl <- 1:39
explvl <- (c(1:10,
            10,10,10,15,20,20,20,25,25,50,
            75,100,125,150,190,200,250,300,350,500,
            500,750,1000,1250,1500,2000,2500,3000,5000) * 1000)

dat <- data.frame(Level = lvl,Exp=explvl)

Exp Curve on the Linear Scale

library(ggplot2)
theme_set(theme_bw())
g <- (ggplot(dat,aes(x=Level,y=Exp))
  + geom_point()
  + geom_smooth()
  + ylab("Experience to Next Level")
)
print(g)

Exp Curve on the Log Scale

g+scale_y_log10()
